E ·Research & Industry Insightsसंशोधन आणि उद्योग

Two research directions, two industry deployments, and one open-source framework relevant to a Marathi-language hospital chatbot.

Research

IndicNLP / IndicBERT family

Ongoing research from AI4Bharat builds pretrained language models and embeddings covering Marathi and other Indic languages, improving downstream tasks like intent classification for low-resource languages.

→ Read the paper (arXiv:2005.00085)

Research

MuRIL (Multilingual Representations for Indian Languages)

A BERT-style model trained specifically on Indian languages including Marathi, useful as a contextual-embedding backbone for a regional-language chatbot without needing to train from scratch.

→ Read the paper (arXiv:2103.10730)

Industry

Practo — AI-based doctor discovery & appointment booking

A commercial Indian healthcare platform using AI/NLP features (symptom-based doctor search, appointment booking, online consultations) at large scale, validating the same problem this project addresses.

→ Visit practo.com

Industry

MyGov Corona Helpdesk — Government of India WhatsApp chatbot

An AI-powered WhatsApp chatbot built by Haptik for the Government of India, handling tens of millions of citizen queries in English and Hindi — a real-world precedent for a regional-language, citizen-facing NLP assistant.

→ Read the case study (indiaai.gov.in)

Framework

Indic NLP Library (open source)

An open-source Python library by Anoop Kunchukuttan / AI4Bharat providing tokenization, normalization, script transliteration, and other text-processing utilities purpose-built for Indian languages including Marathi.

→ View on GitHub
